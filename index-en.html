CRB is a non-profit association that supports the practice of radio modelling in Portugal. It is the largest national radio modelling club, both in number of active members and in number of federated members.

Track

Monsanto Radio
Modelling Track

The Club organises around 12 competition events per year, across various scales from 1/12 to 1/5, with electric and combustion powertrains. Beyond supporting competitive practice, we aim to promote and grow the sport through our Academy, founded in 2024, targeting young people between the ages of 11 and 16.
